How it works

Two ways to find your driver

1
🔍
Browse verified drivers

Open the app, select your route. Scroll through verified driver profiles — 8-category ratings, real car photos, all past reviews. Take your time.

2
📋
Post a trip request

Share your route, date, and passenger count. Drivers bid with their price and a note. Compare bids side by side — profile and price together, always.

3
📞
Pick, call, travel

Found someone you trust? Tap Call. Speak to them directly on your phone. Confirm pickup, agree price. No booking fee. No middleman.

💡
No in-app payments. No GPS tracking. No commission cut.
Intercity trips are planned in advance. You call the driver, agree on price, pay directly by cash or UPI. TaxiYaar owns the trust layer — not the transaction.
