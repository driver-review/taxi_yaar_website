How it works

Two ways to find your driver

1
🔍

Browse verified drivers

Open the app, select your departure city. Scroll through a list of verified drivers on your corridor. Read their full profiles — ratings across 8 categories, real photos of their car, all customer reviews.

2
📋

Or post a trip request

Tell us your route, date, time, and how many passengers. Drivers on that corridor see your request and respond with their price and a personal note. You see all bids side by side — price and full profile together.

3
📞

Pick, call, travel

Found someone you trust? Tap Call. Your phone's native dialer opens. Negotiate the price, confirm the pickup time. No app middleman. No booking fee. Direct connection — just like calling a friend.

💡
No in-app payments. No booking confirmation system. No GPS tracking.
Intercity trips are different from city cabs. You plan them days in advance. You talk to the driver. You pay cash or UPI directly. TaxiYaar's job is the trust layer — not the transaction.

Trust Infrastructure

Every driver on TaxiYaar is a person you'd let into your life.

🪪

Document verification

Aadhaar, driving licence, and RC book checked by a human admin before any profile goes live. No automated approval. A real person reviews every document.

📸

4 car photos — admin verified

Front, side, interior, and boot. You see the actual vehicle you'll spend 6 hours in. Photos are reviewed by our team, not self-attested.

⭐

Real reviews, real people

Reviews are collected via WhatsApp after verified trips — not open to anyone. A driver with 142 reviews earned every single one through 142 actual journeys.

🛡️

24-hour complaint resolution

Any verified complaint triggers immediate admin review. A driver is suspended within 24 hours if a serious incident is confirmed. No exceptions. Reviews are permanent.
